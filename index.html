Style Guide

Developers and designers use design systems in order to organize the look and feel of a website or web application. Design systems help to document the visual vocabulary and reusable components of a large website. This structure not only ensures visual consistency across multiple pages and platforms but also simplifies the development process by providing a clear framework for implementation.

As websites grow larger and more complex, having a consistent set of styles will help them stay cohesive and enjoyable for users! It also helps as one starts working with other developers, as one can have a consistent reference for how your website should be built. In addition, these guidelines promote efficiency, as they reduce the amount of decision-making and rework in the development cycle.
